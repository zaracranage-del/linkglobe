Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

LinkGlobe ("we", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. What We Collect

When you sign up we collect:

  • Your name and email address
  • Your chosen handle and storefront details (bio, social links)
  • Your Amazon affiliate tags (stored securely, used only to build your redirect links)
  • Products you add to your storefront
  • Payment information — processed entirely by Stripe. We never see or store your card details.

2. What We Don't Collect

We do not collect personal information from your fans or visitors. When a fan clicks your LinkGlobe link, we detect their country from their IP address to perform the redirect, but we do not store, log, or profile individual visitor data.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide the geo-redirect service
  • To display your storefront at linkglobe.co/yourhandle
  • To send you account-related emails (magic links, billing)
  • To show you aggregate click analytics on your dashboard

We do not sell your data. We do not use your data for advertising.

4. Data Storage

Your account data is stored securely in Supabase (hosted on AWS in the Sydney region). Payments are handled by Stripe. Both providers are SOC 2 certified.

5. Cookies

We use only essential cookies required for authentication (your login session). We do not use tracking or advertising cookies.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. To request deletion of your account and all associated data, email us at linkglobe.co@gmail.com.

7. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase — database and authentication
  • Stripe — payment processing
  • Vercel — hosting and serverless functions

Each has their own privacy policy which governs their use of data.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. We'll notify you by email for material changes. The date at the top of this page shows when it was last updated.

9. Contact

Questions about your privacy? Email us at linkglobe.co@gmail.com
