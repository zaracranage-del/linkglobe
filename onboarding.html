What is an affiliate tag?

When you join Amazon's affiliate program, they give you a unique tracking code — your tag. Every time a fan buys through a link that includes your tag, Amazon pays you a commission (usually 3–8%).

A product URL with your tag looks like this:

amazon.com.au/dp/B08XYZ?tag=yourname-21

Each country's Amazon is a separate program with a separate tag. That's why we ask for each one — so every fan, wherever they are, earns you commission. You can add more tags any time from your dashboard.

👉 Find your tags: Amazon Associates → Tools → Manage Your Tags
